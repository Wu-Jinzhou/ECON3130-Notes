Big picture
There are many tests of population means:

  • One sample vs. two samples
  • Small sample vs large sample
  • Outcomes can be Normal, binary, or something else
  • Two samples:
    • Independent or paired
    • From populations with equal or unequal variance


We’ve also learned how to test for equality of variances.
